# Ethics and Responsible Use

This repository is intended for paper self-assessment and revision planning only.

## Allowed Uses

- Finding weaknesses before submission.
- Stress-testing claims, experiments, and writing.
- Planning revisions under a deadline.
- Training students to understand peer-review criteria.

## Disallowed Uses

Do not use this prompt to:

- Impersonate real reviewers, SPCs, ACs, or conference officials.
- Claim that the generated review is an official AAAI review.
- Manipulate, attack, probe, or interfere with any real peer-review system.
- Generate fake citations, fake reviewer identities, fake acceptance probabilities, or fake official policies.
- Replace human expert review for high-stakes submission decisions.

## Evidence Standard

The prompt requires the model to ground its criticisms in the provided paper materials. If evidence is unavailable, the model should state that the information is not sufficiently specified rather than guessing.

## Privacy

Do not upload confidential manuscripts, unpublished data, or private reviewer comments to a system unless you have permission and understand the platform's data policy.
